    Stevns Klint

    Stevns Klint is a 17 kilometer long, and up to 41 meter high cliff, which lies at the coast of Stevns. It is recorded in the UNESCO world heritage i 2014. Stevns Klint is the best place in the world, to see and experience the tracks from the astoroid, that collisioned with the earth 66 million years ago.

    Rone Klint Beach

    There are two beaches near Roneklint. One of the beaches lies by Roneklint Skanse and is a sand - and stone beach, mostly consisting of stones. It is a nice place for both kids and adults to snorkel, and you may encounter small fish or eelgrass etc. It is a beautiful place with peace and alot of nature. It is also possible to rent a kayak at Roneklint Kayak Center.

    Read More

    Camp Adventure

    At Camp Adventure you can enjoy a stunning view of the nature and surroundings from the top of a viewing platform, which is located 140 meters above sea level. The trip to, and from, the tower goes through a beautiful scenic route - by a 3,2 kilometers long footpath. You can also visit their climbing park, glamping and flower park

    Feddet

    Visit the beach by Feddet, which is voted as being one of the best beaches on Zealand. Here is a combination of nature and sand dunes. In the high season you can find watersports and activities at the beach. There are alot of exciting activites and natureexperiences at Feddet. Read more - and be amazed by all the things, that you can experience.

    Oremandsgaard Estate

    Come and experience the destillery at Oremandgaards Estate, where they produce gin, whisky and apple brandy. The destillery have a 10 acres large apple orchard with 20 different apple sorts.
