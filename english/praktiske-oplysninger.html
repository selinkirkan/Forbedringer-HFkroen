Directions

The fastest way to get to Hotel Fjordkroen from both South and North, is to follow the E47/E55 freeway. By exit 38 (Tappernøje - Mogenstrup). Drive towards Tappernøje and Præstø (route 209). Turn left (North) 1500m towards the hotel.
It is not possible to use public transport to get to the hotel.

The address is: Bækkeskov Stræde 23, 4733 Tappernøje.

Check-in and out

Check-in is at 15:00-18:00
Check-out is at 11:00
Guests planning on arriving between 18:00 to 21:00, please contact us on the phone.

Should an accident occur, the stay can be canceled up to 48 hours before, at no cost by direct contact to the inn.

For Walking-impaired

The upper floor is only accessible via stairs, there is no elevator on the hotel. Ramps are not available and should be brought.

However, you will be received in the best way possible by our staff.

If you are in a situation of being walking-impaired and want to book a stay, call us and we can figure it out together.
