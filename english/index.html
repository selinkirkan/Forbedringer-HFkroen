    Welcome

    Hotel Fjordkroen is a top-class, 4-star hotel and restaurant.
    Throughout the years, we have received several great awards from Booking.com and Trivago, among others - and have received sky-high reviews from our guests.

    At Hotel Fjordkroen we put the service at the very top, and we do everything we can to ensure, that you as a guest get the best experience.

    Who are we?

    We are a family-run hotel, which is located at the edge of Præstø Fjord - and has been for more than 20 years.

    At Hotel Fjordkroen, we emphasize on giving customers a relaxing and special experience in the most beautiful surroundings.

    We provide good and personal service - as well as delicious food and good wine.
    You can choose to book a table at our restaurant - possibly even with an overnight stay - or book a “Stay” in one of our many fine rooms.

    Restaurant

    At Fjordkroen you will be introduced to our Danish / French cuisine.
    The ingredients are always fresh - and we offer a large selection of fish - and fried dishes, as well as some of the most delicious desserts.

    Get introduced to our large selection of wines and specialty wines, such as
    our tasty Ice wine - imported from Sweden. Visit the restaurant for a unique taste experience.

    In total, our restaurant seats about 60 guests.
